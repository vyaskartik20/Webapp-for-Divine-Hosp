Welcome to
Divine Hospital

Divine Hosptal at Vijay Hata Rd, Siwan, Bihar is one of leading medical clinics in India. We are a multi award winning clinic providing several treatments.

Divine Hospital was established in February 2018 by Dr Ramekbal Prasad Gupta and Dr Kanchan Mala.

Divine Hospital offers 24*7 hours emergency services. We have highly trained staff body consisting of around forty members. The team is highly concerned about the health of the patients and believe in fundamentals of team work. There are private rooms available in sufficient quantities, both AC and non AC rooms are availble according to requirement and preference. The hospital is situated in the heart of the city and is easily accessible.

There are around fifty beds available which cater to the needs of patients. The infrastructure of the hospital is well designed and suited to give the patients a home like feeling. There are general wards and special wards segregation. A lot of services, facilities and treatments are available. We have digital X-Ray and pathology facilities available.

The treatments available include normal delivery, caesrian section, hyscrectomy, IUI (intra uterine), obstrecics and gynacollogy and infertiltiy treatment.

The hospital houses a well equiped pharmacy which cater to all the different types of needs of patients. We, at Divine Hospital try our very best to serve you in every way possible. We care for our patients and help them in quick recovery.

Dr Ramekbal Prasad Gupta completed his M.B.B.S (Hons) and M.S (Ortho) from the prestigious Darbhanga Medical College [DMCH]. He is well renowned consultant orthopaedic surgeon. He completed M.Ch. (gold medalist) from University of Seychelles. He is Ex Senior Residence (Holy Family Hospital New Delhi). He has been doing private practice since the last eleven years. He has vast experience in orthopaedic surgical treatments.

Dr Kanchan Mala completed her M.B.B.S and D.G.O from Darbhanga Medical College [DMCH]. She is consultant gynaecologist and infertility specialist, well known for her work in Bihar. She has been doing private practice since the last four years. She has handled a fair count of critical cases over the years.
